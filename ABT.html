About Me

It is a warm sunny day and I am currently living in a simple life in Pallocan West Batangas, together with my friends. But I am originally came from Arumahan Lemery Batangas. I am the last born in our family, the youngest and they say the precious one. Well I say precious because my mother was having a hard time delivering me; it is nearly in the final destination, means of life and death. Well I can say God is really good because we are kicking and still alive. Here I am living for twenty years and still counting, enjoying every single of life, looking up and dream big and high with my friends.
